Get Dokuen Pro Lifetime for free

Dear Dokuen user,

I believe that education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of financial status. At its core, Dokuen is an educational tool. That's why the base app is completely free, fully functional, and ready to use right out of the box.

The free version includes blazingly fast, on-device ML Kit OCR, which achieves 95+% accuracy on standard typed text like web pages and standard apps (though it struggles with the stylized text found in manga and video games). It also comes with full JMdict and Anki integration, and the ability to import custom dictionaries.

Dokuen Pro, the paid upgrade, unlocks the premium features like options for other OCR engines, specialized dictionaries like JPDB or Kanji-focused dictionaries, alternative machine translation backends, and more. All of these features were built directly in response to user requests.

I think the price I'm charging for Pro Lifetime is fair, especially given the value these advanced tools provide to serious learners. Your financial support helps keep the project going and allows me to dedicate the time needed to keep improving the app. It also allows me to uphold my strict promise to never put ads into Dokuen. I hate ads in apps; they ruin the user experience, break your focus, compromise privacy, and are just plain annoying. That's why you'll never see them here.

While I need to charge for Pro to justify the countless hours of development and keep the lights on, I also know that Dokuen wouldn't be here without its loyal early adopters and beta testers. You all helped lay the foundation for these premium features, and I don't want a price tag to suddenly lock you out of the house you helped build.

I also realize that standards of living differ across the world, and what counts as a reasonable price for some can be unaffordable for others.

If you're an early adopter, or if you've ever submitted a feature request or bug report, or if you simply find these premium features valuable but genuinely cannot afford it, just shoot me an email and I'll send you a promo code to unlock Pro Lifetime for free, no questions asked.

Besides, I know how the internet works. If an app costs money, a cracked APK will eventually show up on some sketchy website. I'd much rather give it to you for free than have you download a hacked version and get a virus.

Other Ways to Give Back

If you can't support the app financially but still want to contribute to the project, there are plenty of incredibly valuable ways to help out:

  • Spread the word: Tell your friends, mention it in language learning communities, or leave an honest review on the Play Store. (BTW, note that your review will not be public if you're in the beta channel. If you happen to be in the beta, and you want your review to be public, you'll have to leave the beta before submitting it. You can rejoin again afterwards.)
  • Write a plugin: If you're a developer, build a custom plugin and make it publicly available to the community.
  • Contribute screenshots: I am always in need of great-looking screenshots of the app in action for the Play Store listing and other marketing material (just make sure they don't contain copyrighted material).
  • Help write guides: I tried to make the app as user-friendly as possible, but feedback suggests many important features remain undiscovered by most users. Writing guides explaining how to use the app helps everyone get the most out of it.

None of this is a requirement, obviously. Whether you decide to pay for Pro or grab it for free, or just keep enjoying the free version, I'm grateful you're here.
