Dokuen Japanese Reader

About this app

Read any Japanese text instantly. Dokuen adds real-time furigana overlays to kanji in any app—perfect for manga, games, news, and websites. Point your camera at real-world text like menus or books to get instant readings.

Unlike a simple translator, Dokuen is built to help you learn and read the original Japanese. Tap any word for a full definition, confirm your understanding with a sentence-level comprehension helper, and send new vocabulary to your Anki deck with a single tap. It's the ultimate all-in-one reading companion.

Main Features

  • 🚀
    Instant Screen Overlay Mode

    Activate the overlay to get real-time furigana in any app! Perfect for reading news, social media, games, and your favorite e-book or manga apps.

  • 📸
    Powerful Camera Mode (OCR)

    Point your camera at any physical text—a restaurant menu, a sign, or your Japanese books—and see furigana appear like magic. Perfect for practicing your reading in the real world.

  • 📖
    Tap-to-Define Integrated Dictionary

    Build your vocabulary as you read. Tap on any word with a furigana overlay to get its definition, reading, and other details from our comprehensive built-in dictionary.

  • 🔍
    Comprehension Helper

    Need more context? Simply drag-select an entire sentence to view its translation. This tool is designed as a learning aid to confirm your reading, not to replace it.

  • 📇
    One-Tap Anki Integration

    Supercharge your studies! Found a new word? Add it directly to your Anki flashcard decks from within the app. Learning new vocabulary has never been more streamlined.

  • 📶
    Flexible Offline & Online Modes

    Read and study anywhere, anytime. The app works perfectly offline for total convenience. For situations requiring the highest possible accuracy, you can enable an optional online mode to enhance text recognition.

  • ⚙️
    Highly Customizable

    Your reading, your way. Easily adjust the font size and color of the furigana to create the perfect reading experience that's easy on your eyes.
